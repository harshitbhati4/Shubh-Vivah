Meeting to Engagement (M2E)

Shubh Vivah will arrange suitable meetings for bride and groom based upon various parameters. However meetings can be successful or failure depending upon how the meetings are handled. Shubh Vivah's experience & enrichment services provide prospective bride and groom and their families, coaching and analysis on the following areas:

  • Personality and behavior analysis for successful meetings leading to engagement.
  • Grooming and etiquette for successful meetings leading to engagements.
  • Social skills and interaction analysis and coaching for successful meetings leading to engagements.

Engagement to Marriage (E2M)

Shubh Vivah will provide guidance, analysis and coaching on how to ensure the period of soft companionship is not disturbed due to any personality and behavior, grooming and etiquette and social skills. The benefit will be to ensure successful marriage. Shubh Vivah's experience & enrichment services provide prospective bride and groom coaching and analysis on the following areas:

  • Personality and behavior analysis for successful interactions post engagement leading to marriages.
  • Grooming and etiquette for successful interactions post engagement leading to marriages.
  • Social skills and interaction analysis and coaching post engagement leading to successful marriages.

Who should take it?

  • All Bride and Groom and families who are planning to get married involving family
